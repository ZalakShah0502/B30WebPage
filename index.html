What is B30?

B30 is a biofuel blend containing 30% biodiesel (fatty acid methyl ester) mixed with 70% petroleum diesel. The biodiesel component is typically derived from renewable sources like palm oil, soybean oil, or used cooking oil.

This blend represents a significant step up from B20, offering greater environmental benefits while maintaining compatibility with most diesel engines designed for lower blends.

Important Considerations

Engine Compatibility

May require engine modifications or OEM approval. Check manufacturer guidelines before use.

Cold Weather Performance

Biodiesel can gel in cold temperatures. Cold flow additives may be needed in winter.

Storage Requirements

Biodiesel degrades faster than petroleum diesel. Proper storage conditions are essential.

Fuel System Maintenance

B30 has solvent properties that may loosen deposits. More frequent filter changes may be needed initially.
